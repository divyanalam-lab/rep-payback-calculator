Org CAC = monthly rep cost ÷ orgs closed per month at steady state. Org LTV = total referrer-months over org lifetime × leads/ref × lead CVR × patient LTV. Patient LTV = net rev/patient × GM × sum of retention curve.
Model: Rep ramp (4 mo) → QP generation → org close (lagged) → referrer adoption (6 mo curve) → 3-bucket MAR (new / gross retained / winback) → lead generation → patient conversion → patient retention (24-mo curve) → revenue → GP accumulation vs cost.
Referrer churn: Total MAR[t] = New[t] + MAR[t-1] × gross retention + MAR[t-1] × winback rate. Equivalent to: New[t] + MAR[t-1] × net retention.
